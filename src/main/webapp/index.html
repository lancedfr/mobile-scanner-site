Dietitian Shopper

We thought on how to make the shopping experience more convenient for these shoppers. The first solution was to make a website with a product table and people with illnesses that cannot consume it. The solution however became redundant as this will take just as long for the user to find the product in the list.

As the mobile phone has become more of a necessity then a wallet, we decided to come up with a scanner application that utilizes the phones camera utility. The user can find out if the product is harmful to him, simply by scanning the barcode.

About

The concept of this application came to the group when we spoke about food, and how most people do not like shopping. Also how some illnesses like heart disease and diabetes causes even more inconvenience for the shopper. These illnesses means that the shopper will spend a few hours more in the shop just reading the ingredients to determine if they can consume it.
